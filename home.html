DeckLog

The squadron whenever they are sailing would record their coordinates and information using pen and paper. They specifically requested Android Application and within 12 hours we delivered that promise. During CodeJam, our team designed and developed the application called DeckLog from scratch using Android Studio with SQLite as the back-end. The app uses a map API that tracks the user's location and forms the users use to keep track of their sails. All of the logs saved, will be store in our database which we made using SQLite.

  • Client: Redwood Power & Sail Squadron (Kingston)
  • Date: February 2016
  • Type: Android Application

MakAttack

MakAttack is a PC/Mac/Linux game made on Unity for the course CISC226: Game Design. MakAttack lets you control the protagonist been contracted to kill King Garth, and to get to Garth, you must get through his castle and defeat knights. The novel aspect of this game is to complete each level fast by finding easter eggs that let's you skip certain part of a level.
